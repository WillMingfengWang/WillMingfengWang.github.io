About Me

Date: December 20, 2021  14:37:31

Currently, I am a Ph.D candidate at Institute of Applied Mechanics, University of Stuttgart. My academic research interests is to investigate how fluid flow in porous media by means of experiments and simulation methods. The main experimental method is high-resolution X-Ray CT, FIB-SEM and SEM, and therefore I am also interested in image/date processing by using matlab, python and Avizo, a powerful image processing software. In order to carry out numerical simulation, CFD also interests me and I mainly use Finite Volume Method (OpenFoam) to perform simulation.

If you are interested in my research, please feel free to contact me.
