1 Nabla Operator

1.1 WHY

\(\nabla\cdot(\varphi\vec{F})=\varphi\nabla\cdot\vec{F}+\vec{F}\cdot\nabla\varphi\)

1.2 ANSWER

There are several reasons:

  1. \(\nabla\) is the vector and differential operator which means it has differential properties, and then the operation should obey the differential operation rules
  2. \(\nabla\) only works on vector
